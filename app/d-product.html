Description

Let Me Tell You What Happens Next, 2019 was influenced by my interest in spirituality, magic and astrology, featuring various symbols and motifs from tarot cards. Two completely different worlds collapse into one another in this vase: Russian folkloric motifs such as monograms and traditional ornate patterns that are integrated with imagery from my local South London neighbourhood. Both are infused with different kinds of visceral nostalgia and symbolic significance. The “third eye” or mind’s eye on this work speculates the possibilities within the realm of dreams, the subconscious and alternative, mediumistic predictions of the future.
